About

A specialist outsourced engineering partner

UH Consulting helps engineering teams adopt model-based systems engineering, strengthen architecture clarity, and connect digital engineering practices to real product deliverables.

Our approach

We are a Canadian consultancy based in Vancouver, BC, focused on systems and digital engineering. We work alongside client teams — not as a detached drafting service — to improve how complex products are architected, specified, verified, and delivered.

MBSE-first consulting

Our engagements lead with model-based systems engineering and architecture practices. CAD and mechanical design support complements systems work when execution deliverables are needed.

SysML 2.0 expertise

We help teams adopt SysML 2.0 modeling conventions, define repository structures, and build models that support traceability and stakeholder communication.

Practical engineering experience

Our background spans mechatronics and mechanical engineering, enabling us to bridge systems models with CAD workflows, interfaces, and physical design considerations.

Embedded team collaboration

We integrate with client engineering teams on-site or remotely, adapting to existing processes and toolchains rather than imposing one-size-fits-all methods.

Traceability & lifecycle focus

We emphasize requirements traceability, configuration management, and verification planning to support programs through design milestones and beyond.

Architecture clarity

Clear system architecture reduces ambiguity across disciplines, supports trade studies, and provides a stable foundation for digital thread initiatives.
